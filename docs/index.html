A Chrome extension that adds full-text search and bookmarking to ChatGPT. Never lose an important conversation again.

Instant Search

Press ⌘+S to search through all your conversations. Find any message or topic in seconds.

⭐

Smart Bookmarks

Click the star icon to bookmark important conversations. Organize them into custom folders.

Privacy First

All data stays local in your browser. No external servers, no data collection.
